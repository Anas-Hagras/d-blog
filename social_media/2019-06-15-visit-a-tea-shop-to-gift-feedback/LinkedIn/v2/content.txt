Harnessing the power of feedback is an unparalleled investment in both personal and professional growth. A well-crafted feedback loop not only strengthens those around you but also elevates your own trajectory. 

Consider this template for delivering impactful feedback: 
1. I observed...
2. It made me feel...
3. One idea to improve is...

Remember, feedback should be timely, fact-based, and empathetic.
