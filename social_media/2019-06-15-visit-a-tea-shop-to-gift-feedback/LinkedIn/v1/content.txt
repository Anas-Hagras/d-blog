Giving impactful feedback is more than a skill; it's a game-changer. Constructive feedback not only strengthens the individual but also elevates the entire team. Here's a simple yet powerful template: 

1. **I observed...**
2. **It made me feel...**
3. **One idea to improve is...**

Pair this with the "A TEA SHOPS" framework to assess strengths: Athlete, Teacher, Engineer, etc.
